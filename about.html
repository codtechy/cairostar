About Company

Cairo star ship supply is an international company, offering our clients with unique service of high quality products and services.

Cairo Star Ship Chandlers Established in 2002 U.A.E.

CAIRO STAR SHIP CHANDLERS is a leading name in the ship chandelling business today and is recognized as a one stop destination offering an extensive range of premium quality products & services in the field of marine and logistics industry. Headquartered in United Arab Emirates, CAIRO STAR SHIP CHANDLERS today is a name to reckon with as an importer, exporter, distributor & ship chandler for a complete range of products for the ship stores. With a fully enabled and resourceful warehouse and logistics operations, CAIRO STAR SHIP CHANDLERS can cater to any ship supply demand.

We offer a fine range of products including dry, chilled and frozen products catering to the needs of the ship chandelling industry. We provide quality products for all shipboard needs including hardware products.

Armed with immense knowledge of ship stores, CAIRO STAR SHIP CHANDLERS has consistently aimed to achieve the highest standard in service to all customers and has gained its place as one of the most recognized and trusted names in the marine-supply industry. Our commitment towards quality and timely delivery, coupled with ethical business policies, sound understanding of customer requirements has earned us the trust of our esteemed clients.

Cairo star complying strictly with international foods standards We have the highest compliance and quality food safety standards. We are always striving to minimise operational risk related to food supply by complying strictly with international food standards, and our integrated management system has been certified globally according to ISO 9001:2008 and ISO 14001:2004.
